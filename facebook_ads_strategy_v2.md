# Solace Skin Lab — Facebook & Instagram Ads Strategy V2

**Period:** July 24–August 22, 2026  
**Location:** Cielo Vista Mall, El Paso, TX  
**Official phone:** (915) 995-9524  
**Status:** Client review required before activation

## Monthly objective

Generate qualified calls and consultation inquiries while building a reusable local retargeting audience. Optimize toward qualified inquiries and confirmed bookings—not clicks alone.

## Recommended budget distribution

- **50% Local prospecting:** facials, LED and consultation-first brand awareness.
- **30% Offer conversion:** luxury skincare/free facial and Tuesday 2×1.
- **20% Retargeting:** recent Facebook/Instagram engagers, video viewers and website visitors.

Percentages allocate the client-approved monthly total; they do not authorize an increase in total spend.

## Four-week flight

1. **Jul 24–30 — Trust and audience building:** launch brand/facial and LED ads; build engagement pools.
2. **Jul 31–Aug 6 — First conversion push:** launch skincare/free-facial and Tuesday 2×1; add BOTOX® Cosmetic only after regulatory review.
3. **Aug 7–13 — Consultation intent:** introduce microneedling and JUVÉDERM® consultation ads; retarget weeks 1–2 engagement.
4. **Aug 14–22 — Retargeting and final opportunity:** rotate second skincare offer, final Tuesday creative and general consultation ad; move allocation toward qualified-inquiry winners.

## Audience architecture

- Broad local adults within a practical service radius of Cielo Vista Mall.
- Wine-related offer delivery restricted to **age 21+**.
- Retarget recent Facebook/Instagram engagers, video viewers and website visitors when available.
- Exclude confirmed recent bookings or converted leads when reliable consented lists exist.
- Avoid audiences and copy that infer sensitive health conditions, insecurities or diagnoses.

## Testing and optimization

- Test one major variable at a time: copy, creative or audience.
- Use the approved square creative in feeds; QA any Story/Reel crop before activation.
- Measure qualified calls, consultation inquiries and confirmed bookings first.
- Use CTR and engagement as diagnostic signals, not the sole winner criteria.
- Rotate creative when frequency rises and response quality falls.
- Reallocate within the approved monthly total; do not increase total spend without approval.
- Add Meta Pixel/Conversions API and UTMs if the approved destination supports them.
